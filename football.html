Jeremiah Pharms

2001 5th round Cleveland Brown draft pick Jeremiah Pharms joins Dante and Ryan to talk about his experiences in life, from being a star at the University of Washington, to being an NFL draft pick locked up behind bars.

Arland Bruce III

Arland Bruce III joins Dante and Ryan to talk about his experiences in college, the NFL, his CFL career, and what he has been up to after football.

Milt Stegall

CFL legend Milt Stegall joins Dante Marsh and Ryan Heid to talk about his career in the CFL, playing in the NFL, missed opportunities to win a Grey Cup, and who the best all time NBA player is. Dante and Milt also talk about their battles against each other on the field, and practicing together in the off season in Atlanta.

Casey Printers

Casey talks about how he got started playing football, playing college ball with Texas Christian University, and then transferring to Florida A&M. Casey then went on to play with the BC Lions, Hamilton Ti-Cats, and Kansas City Chiefs. Casey also talks about the challenges of being a pro athlete, life after football, and the Black Lives Matter movement. This is one episode you won't want to miss!

Ricky Manning Jr.

In this episode, Dante and Ryan interview ex NFL player Ricky Manning Jr. Ricky talks about going to Edison High School in Fresno, being a Minnesota Twins prospect, choosing football over baseball, attending UCLA, playing in Super Bowl 38, and being a coach with the Seattle Seahawks, amongst other things. We had a blast recording this episode, and we are sure that you'll enjoy listening to it!

Korey Banks

Korey Banks joins us on this episode of Inside the Minds. Korey talks about his time with the Mississippi State Bulldogs, the Washington Redskins, Miami Dolphins, and then making his way to the CFL to play with the Ottawa Renegades, and the BC Lions. Korey is a 2 time Grey Cup Champion, 4 time CFL All-Star, 7 time CFL West All-Star, and 1 time CFL East All-Star.

Carl Kidd

Carl spent 2 seasons with the Oakland Raiders, before heading North to play 6 seasons with the BC Lions, where he was a 2 time All-Star, and appeared in 3 Grey Cups, winning 2. Carl is also a member of the BC Lions Wall of Fame.

Stephen Adekolu

In this episode, Dante and Ryan have Stephen Adekolu on as their guest. Stephen played 6 seasons in the Canadian Football League, and is now an actor in movies and t.v. shows. Stephen talks about his childhood, going to Bishop's University, his time in the CFL with the B.C. Lions and the Montreal Aloutettes, Systemic Racism, being an advocate for violence against women, and how he got his acting career started. Stephen also does a little improv during the show!

Aaron Hunt

Aaron Hunt joins Dante and Ryan on the show and talks about growing up with his brother Reggie, playing High School football in Texas, playing College football with Texas Tech, being drafted by the Denver Broncos, playing in the NFL Europe, and playing with the BC Lions. Aaron was the CFL's Most Outstanding Rookie in 2006, a 2 time Grey Cup Champion, a 2 time CFL All-Star, and a 5 time CFL West All-Star.

Chris Rainey

Chris Rainey is the guest on Inside the Minds Podcast. Chris talks about training during the Covid Pandemic, growing up in Lakeland Florida, playing on the 2009 Champion Florida Gators, the 2012 NFL Draft, his time in the NFL, coming to Canada to play in the CFL, and the potential merger between the CFL and XFL.

Jovon Johnson

The 2011 CFL's Most Outstanding Defensive Player Award Winner and 2007 Grey Cup Champ Jovon Johnson is this weeks guest. He was a 2 time CFL All-Star, and a 5 time CFL East All-Star. Jovon talks about how it all got started in Erie, Pennsylvania, playing college football at Iowa State, his time in the NFL, and then going on to Canada to play in the CFL. He is now a DB Coach at Defiance College.

Logan Bandy

Dante Marsh and Ryan Heid interview the Saskatchewan Roughriders 38th overall pick in the 2021 CFL draft, Logan Bandy. Logan talks about playing hockey as a youth, choosing Football over Hockey, winning the 2019 Vanier Cup with the Calgary Dino's, his expectations heading into the 2021 CFL Draft, his Combine experience, being drafted by the Saskatchewan Roughriders, what it takes to achieve your goals, and being a life long Calgary Flames fan.

Geroy Simon

Dante and Ryan interview CFL Hall of Famer Geroy Simon about playing NCAA football with Maryland, his time in the NFL, coming to Canada to play in the CFL, becoming the all-time leader in most career recieving yards, winning 3 Grey Cups, and what he's been up to after retiring as a player.

Nelson and Boseko Lokombo

Nelson and Boseko Lokombo join the show to talk about the upcoming 2021 CFL season.

Chris Caulk

former USC walk on Wide Receiver Chris Caulk joins Ryan to talk about his journey to USC Football, battling through the naysayers, and following his dreams!
